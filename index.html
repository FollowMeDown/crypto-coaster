Crypto Coaster

Hey! My name is Harvey Delaney and I wrote Crypto Coaster to improve my skills with JavaScript.

This is one of a few of my small projects I write to get better at coding. Check my other projects out on my website.

It started off as a very small project with only 3 coins, Bitcoin, Ethereum and Litecoin. As people gave great feedback, I spent more time improving the look and feel of the site as well as adding 6 more coins.

I plan to add more coins in the future as I find/create the appropriate coaster gifs for other currencies.

If you have any requests, or want to contribute yourself. Use the contact me page on my main portfolio.
